About the Business

Built to make local tutoring simple, trustworthy, and easy to access.

We focus on connecting families with offline tutors who understand the local neighborhoods, school needs, and parent expectations.

Who We Are

StudyConnectIndia is a local tutoring support platform created for families who prefer one-to-one offline tuition at home. Our MVP is designed to simplify tutor discovery, inquiry management, and local tutor onboarding.

Why Choose Us

We focus on local coverage, subject-specific matches, responsive communication, and a simple inquiry flow. Families can share their needs quickly and tutors can register without complex onboarding.

Mission

To help students receive personalized academic support at home through trusted and accessible offline tutoring.

Vision

To become a reliable local bridge between families and qualified tutors across growing cities and neighborhoods in India.
